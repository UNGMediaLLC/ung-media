Who We Are

ALEX URIKH

Alex Urikh hasn't been able to watch a movie normally in years. Every cut, every color grade, every sound decision — his brain is already pulling it apart before the scene ends. How did they hide that? Why does this feel so heavy? What are they not showing you?

It started with a scene from the Miami Vice pilot — Phil Collins fading in, two men who don't say a word, neon barely catching their faces. No wind. Just an engine. That scene didn't teach him about film. It told him what he was supposed to do with his life.

Observation, obsession, and something that's really yours.

That's how UNG Media works.

UNG Media works at the intersection of craft and technology. AI tools are woven into the production process — not to replace instinct, but to sharpen it. Faster turnarounds, sharper concepts, and more time spent on what actually matters: making the shot land.

Human eye. AI backbone. No compromises.
